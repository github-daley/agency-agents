# Thriving Through Menopause — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
**Thriving Through Menopause** is Australia's premier boutique symposium dedicated to advancing women's health through the menopause transition. It brings together world-class medical professionals, wellness experts, and industry leaders to deliver evidence-based insights in an intimate, empowering setting.

**Website**: https://thrivingthroughmenopause.com.au
**Founder/Curator**: Chiza Westcarr
**Venue**: State Library of NSW, Sydney
**Style**: Boutique-style conference
**Target audience**: Healthcare professionals, wellness practitioners, and women seeking expert guidance through perimenopause, menopause, and postmenopause.

## Imagery Style
- **Photography**: Real women 40s–60s, confident, active, joyful — not stock-photo-sad
- **Setting**: Natural light, warm tones, premium interiors, nature elements
- **Avoidance**: Clinical/cold imagery, stereotypical "old age" tropes, anything that feels pitying
- **Mood**: Radiant, empowered, connected, intelligent

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use warm, empowering language
- Show diverse women thriving
- Lead with education and expertise
- Create gentle urgency ("limited seats") without pressure tactics
- Celebrate this life stage as transformative

### ❌ Don't
- Use clinical, cold, or scary language about menopause
- Use stock photos of sad or suffering women
- Exaggerate urgency ("BUY NOW!")
- Use overly bright/loud colors that feel mass-market
- Talk down to the audience
