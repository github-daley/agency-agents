### A/B Test Variations to Run
1. **Headline test**: Question ("Ready to thrive?") vs Statement ("Where women come to thrive")
2. **CTA test**: "Get Tickets" vs "Secure Your Seat" vs "Join Us"
3. **Visual test**: Quote graphic vs speaker headshots vs venue photography
4. **Audience test**: Interest-based vs lookalike vs retargeting

### Tracking Parameters

Examples:
- `?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=ttm2026&utm_content=ad1_awareness`
- `?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=ttm2026&utm_content=healthcare_professionals`
