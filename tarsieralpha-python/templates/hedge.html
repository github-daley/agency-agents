{# ── How It Works ── #}
How Hedge Mode Works
1. Industry Mapping
We identify publicly-traded companies whose stock price is highly correlated with costs in your industry (e.g., diesel = XPO, JBHT, ODFL).
2. Short Scoring
Each correlated ticker is scored for short setup quality (RSI, BB, volume fade). Only candidates scoring ≥70 make it into the basket.
3. PUT Sizing
Position size = hedge target ÷ basket size. Contracts = (per-position USD / 100) / put premium. Estimated gain uses delta × 5% price drop.
